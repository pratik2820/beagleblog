The Beagle Blog

The Beagle is a breed of small scent hound,similar in apaperance to much larger foxhound.The beagle was developed primarily for hunting hare, known as beagling.Possesing a great sense of smell and superior tracking insticts, the beagle is the primary breed used as a detection dog for prohibited agricultural imports and foodstuffs in quarantine around the world.The beagle is intelligent and is a popular pet due to its size,good temper,and a lack of inherited health problems.
